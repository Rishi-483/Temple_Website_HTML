Our History

Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple is located in Vadapalli, Atreyapuram Mandal, East Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh. This ancient and revered temple is situated on the scenic banks of the river Godavari, about 10 km from Ravulapalem, a major local junction.

The presiding deity, Sri Venkateswara Swamy, also known as Kalyana Venkateswara, is uniquely crafted from red sandalwood. The temple is especially popular among devotees for fulfilling wishes related to marriage and prosperity.
